Data Analysis of Unanswered Requests from ABA Clients

ASA Datafest at Duke 2023

By: Amber Shuang; Dan Peng; Feifan Yang; Mingzhe Zhou and Yukai Li

Introduction

This study investigates the potential variables that affect unanswered questions in a dataset, with particular emphasis on four key variables: 1) overall categories, 2) categories by state, 3) income of clients with unanswered questions, and 4) age of clients with unanswered questions. The objective of this research is to identify potential patterns or associations between these variables and the incidence of unanswered questions. The results of this study may provide insights into factors that contribute to incomplete data in the dataset, and inform strategies for addressing these issues in future data collection efforts.
